      About BudgetWise

      BudgetWise

      Smart Budgeting. Smarter Future.

      BudgetWise is a premium, privacy-first personal finance tracker. Every rupee you log stays on your own device — there's no server, no account, and no tracking. Add income and expenses, set category budgets and savings goals, and see your financial picture come together in real time through clean, focused charts.

      Built with

      • HTML5, CSS3 & Vanilla JavaScript (ES6)
      • Chart.js for analytics
      • Browser Local Storage for data persistence
